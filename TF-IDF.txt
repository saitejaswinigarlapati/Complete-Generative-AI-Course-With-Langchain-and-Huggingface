Term Frequency - Inverse Document Frequency

TF= (No.of repeating words in sentence)/ No.of words in sentence

IDF= log(No.of sentences/No.of sentences containg the word)

Example:
S1: good boy
S2: good girl
S3: boy girl good

Term Frequence:                          IDF
        S1      S2      S3              Word    IDF
good   1/2     1/2     1/3              good    log(3/3)=0
boy    1/23     0      1/3              boy     log(3/2)
girl    0      1/2     1/3              girl    log(3/2)


TF- IDF:

TF-IDF= TF * IDF
        good    boy             girl
S1      0       1/2 *log(3/2)   0
S2      0       0               1/2 log(3/2)
S3      0       1/3log(3/2)     1/3log(3/2)

Advantages:

1. Intuitive
2. Input is fixed size
3. Word importance is getting capture

Disadvantages:

1. Sparsity still exists
2. Out of vocubulary
